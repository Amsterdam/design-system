### On a coloured background

A link on a coloured background must adjust its text colour to provide enough contrast.
We have lighter and darker background colours, and links behave differently on each.

- Set the `color` prop to `contrast` on a azure, lime, orange or yellow background.
  It will make the Link black.
- Set the `color` prop to `inverse` on a blue, green, magenta, purple or red background.
  It will make the Link white.
